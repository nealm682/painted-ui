# Flipbook launch thread — @zan2434 (Zain Shah)

- **Source type:** X/Twitter launch thread
- **Author:** Zain Shah (@zan2434), with Eddie Jiao and Drew Carr
- **Launched:** April 2026
- **Captured:** 2026-07-18 (seed facts as provided by Neal; immutable)

## Key public facts

- Flipbook streams **live 1080p video at 24fps**, generated by a
  heavily-optimized version of **LTX Studio's video model**.
- Delivery: frames streamed **over websockets**.
- Infrastructure: **Modal** serverless GPU infra.
- Core selling points:
  - **No HTML, no layout engine** — every pixel is generated by the model.
  - **Illustrations reshape to fit the window** (fluid, resolution-independent
    feel).
  - **Any region can become interactive** — interactivity is not limited to
    predefined widgets.
- Admitted limitations (stated openly by the team):
  - Early and **slow**.
  - **Demos were sped up / edited.**
  - **Scaling/capacity problems** — waiting rooms for access.
