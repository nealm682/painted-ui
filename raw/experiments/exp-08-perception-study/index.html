A short comparison study

You'll see three versions (A, B, C) of the same small interface, one at a time. Each one will show you a travel scene and ask you to click one element. After each version, you'll rate your impressions on seven quick scales.

The three versions are shown in random order. There are no right answers — rate what you felt, not what you think is expected. Takes about 3 minutes.
